The Elden Ring
Shadow of the Erdtree DLC date










The Elden Ring Shadow of the Erdtree DLC has finally been properly revealed with a glorious trailer
showing off the Land of Shadow and a June release. This land is the setting for the expansion and is its own map,
where ye Tarnished players will be following in the footsteps of Miquella
to learn why he came to this mysterious land. It's the first (and likely only) expansion for Elden Ring,
and it looks pretty massive with a huge variety of open areas and dungeons to delve into.
Of course there will be some horrific bosses waiting in those spaces too. Let's take you through everything we know
about Elden Ring DLC and the Shadow of the Erdtree expansion.






You can play Shadow of the Erdtree on June 21, 2024. Pre-orders are now live,
including a swanky collector's edition that comes with an art book and a
massive statue of new character Messmer the Impaler.
Shadow of the Erdtree isn't a standalone thing, so you will need to own Elden Ring already
to play the expansion, but you can also get a convenient bundle that includes both.
